Our Section

"Always encouraging women to succeed!"

The University of Central Florida section of the Society of Women Engineers is a section of the national organization serving Central Florida. The primary purpose of SWE is to help students at UCF become aware of the opportunities available to them in engineering and STEM related careers. SWE empowers women and men to aspire, advance, succeed, and be recognized for their life-changing contributions and achievements as engineers and leaders.

Our section hosts different events each month to allow members to network with professionals, meet other women in engineering, and become involved in community outreach.

UCF SWE Outreach

"A small definition of what this experience is about!"

SWE at UCF hosts two major outreach events and attends countless more. We work with the UCF College of Engineering and Computer Science, the Orlando Science Center, the Girl Scouts of America, and other organizations to encourage girls to explore STEM. At our two signature events, we host workshops that explore the different fields of engineering and connect students and parents with industry professionals so they can learn what it is really like to be an engineer. In addition, SWE at UCF supports SWENext school clubs in our area.
