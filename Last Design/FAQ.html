Membership

"Benefits, internships, scholarships!"

Do I need to pay dues to be a member of UCF SWE?

No! You can pay to become to be a member of National SWE and mark UCF as your section, but you only need to do this to become an officer or attend conference with us.



I can’t make a general body meeting. Can I still be a member of UCF SWE?

Yes! You can attend as many or as few meetings as you want, and we have events at other times that you can also attend. Follow us on Facebook or sign up to our Newsletter in the home page to be up-to-date on our events.



I’m a graduate student. Can I still be a member of UCF SWE?

Yes! There is no undergraduate requirement.



I’m an international student. Can I still be a member of UCF SWE?

Yes! However, if you want to attend National Conference with UCF SWE, please mark your international status on your application and contact the conference chair because there might be extra fees.



I’m a student who is going to transfer to UCF. Can I attend SWE events at UCF now?

Yes! Follow us on Facebook or sign up to our Newsletter in the home page to be up-to-date on our events.



How do I become an officer of UCF SWE?

During the Spring semester, we will send out officer applications for the upcoming school year. Fill one out and return it to apply. You need to be a member of National SWE.



Outreach

"A learning environment for all!"

How do I volunteer in UCF SWE outreach events?

Stay up to date on outreach opportunities with the Newsletter in the home page or our Facebook page. You can also email swe.ucf.outreach@gmail.com.

What is SWENext?

SWENext is a way to become part of the Society of Women Engineers as a student through the age of 18. Joining is free. Any student 13 or older can become a SWENexter. Please click here to find out more!

I’m a middle school or high school student or teacher and I want to attend a UCF SWE outreach event. Where do I find more information?

Please check out our Outreach page in this website or email swe.ucf.outreach@gmail.com.

I’m a middle school or high school student or teacher and I want to start a SWE Next club. What do I need to do?

We love to work with local SWENext clubs! If you’re trying to find a member of SWE to be an advisor or to partner with us on events, you can reach out to us here. Otherwise, have all of your members join SWENext and then register your club here

I am the parent of a middle school or high school student. Can I attend outreach events hosted by UCF SWE?

Yes! We try to put a separate program together for parents to help provide them with resources about college and an engineering career path. There should be a parent sign up for UCF SWE outreach events. You can contact our outreach chairs at swe.ucf.outreach@gmail.com.

Conference

"Internships, networking, and more!"

Do I need to apply to go to the National SWE Conference with UCF SWE?

Yes, applications for attending conference in the UCF SWE group are sent out in the early Fall semester.

Do I need to pay money to go to the National SWE Conference with UCF SWE?

Yes, the cost can be around $100-$300, depending on the conference location, and does not include meals. UCF SWE will cover registration, hosuing, and the flight.

Do I need to be a member of National SWE to go to the National SWE Conference with UCF SWE?

Yes, you are required to be a member of National SWE to attend conference in the UCF SWE group.

I’m an international student. Can I go to the National SWE Conference with UCF SWE?

Yes! However, please mark your international status on your application and contact the conference chair because there might be extra fees.

Can I go to the National or Local SWE Conference by myself, without going in a group with UCF SWE?

Yes! Our conference officers can help you apply to receive funding from UCF SGA and can provide assisstance if you need any help registering. Email them at swe.ucf.conference@gmail.com.
