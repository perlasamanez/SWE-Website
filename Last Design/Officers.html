Our officers 2017-2018

Thanks to this amazing team, SWE at UCF keeps being an awesome organization!

Kelsie McCall

President

Industrial Engineering, Senior

"I have been a member of SWE since I was a freshman and an officer for the past three years. I received my last internship at the National SWE conference, and I want to share the opportunities I’ve had and the friends I’ve made through SWE with other women."

Contact me:

swe.ucf.president@gmail.com

Christina Hagarty

Vice President

Mechanical Engineering, Junior

"I have been interning at the Naval Surface Warfare Center in Philadelphia, PA for the last four summers. My goal is to get more women involved in SWE and within the STEM fields. I'm excited to work with our members to help them in their professional careers."

Contact me:

swe.ucf.vicepresident@gmail.com

Michaela Pain

Secretary

Computer Engineering, Junior

"I have made SWE a priority in my collegiate career since my freshman year. I'm looking forward being a part of the executive board. My goal this year is to recruit and retain more members to SWE and as always, encourage more women to pursue engineering disciplines."

Contact me:

swe.ucf@gmail.com

Jessica Koppenhofer

Treasurer

Industrial Engineering, Junior

Contact me:

swe.ucf.treasurer@gmail.com
