National SWE

The Society of Women Engineers is a National organization who's mission is to "stimulate women to achieve full potential in careers as engineers and leaders, expand the image of the engineering profession as a positive force in improving the quality of life, and demonstrate the value of diversity." Click here to become a member of the National Society, to use SWE's learning resources, to apply for National scholarships, and to explore the achievements and opportunities SWE has to offer.



Central Florida SWE

The Central Florida Section of SWE is the local Orlando professional chapter. They work closely with SWE at UCF to further support the mission of encouraging the professional growth of engineers through networking and learning opportunities. The professional section volunteers to host resume workshops and mock interviews for our members at UCF to help better prepare for future job opportunities. To learn more about Central Florida SWE's events, volunteer opportunities, scholarships, job postings, and how to get involved just click here!

"Some resources we work with to help you succeed at UCF!"

CECS Office of Diversity and Inclusion

The College of Engineering and Computer Science Office of Diversity and Inclusion is a unique resource for minority, women, and underrepresented students in engineering and computer science at UCF. It's purpose is to foster growth and diversity by hosting a wide variety of programs including presentations from multinational companies through its IGNITE lecture series, research and mentoring opportunities for undergraduate students through RAMA, and ACADA talks highlighting graduate research. CECS ODI also works with SARC to provide tutoring for specific UCF undergraduate courses every semester. Click here to learn more about the programs, scholarships, and research and mentoring opportunities provided by CECS ODI.



UCF Career Services

"UCF Career Services contributes to the university's goal of offering high quality undergraduate and graduate education and student development by providing centralized, comprehensive and coordinated career development, experiential learning and employer relations programs." UCF Career Services provides a plethora of resources for UCF students to help them jump-start their careers: resume workshops, mock interviews, career and internship fairs, networking workshops, and so much more. To learn more about the UCF Career Services programs that can best help you plan your future career, click here!
